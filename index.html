How it works

Sensors monitor soil moisture, weather and trigger automated drip irrigation; biodegradable hydrogel retains water and releases it to roots — reducing irrigation frequency and improving resilience.

Pilot: UDS Nyankpala
Partners: Saba Innovation Hub

About SE‑SOFJEL

SE‑SOFJEL (SmartFarm) is a climate‑smart agritech social enterprise that integrates IoT irrigation systems with a locally developed biodegradable hydrogel (UDS Spanish Lab) to enable sustainable, year‑round farming in semi‑arid regions.

Technology & Solution

Smart Irrigation

Soil moisture, humidity, and rain sensors automate drip irrigation — conserving water and improving crop health.

Biodegradable Hydrogel

Locally produced hydrogel stores water in the soil and releases it slowly, improving water retention and root growth.

Training & Support

Farmer training, demo farms, and technical support through UDS and Saba Innovation Hub ensure adoption and sustainability.

SDG Impact

SE‑SOFJEL supports 16 of the 17 UN SDGs (excluding SDG 14) by promoting food security, job creation, gender inclusion, water efficiency, climate resilience and sustainable land use.
